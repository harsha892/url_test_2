About us

DEET - Digital Employment Exchange of Telangana is an initiative launched by the Government of Telangana to improve the employment ecosystem in the state. DEET eliminates the hurdles leading to a friction-free interaction between job seekers and job providers. Powered AI & ML tech stack, the results obtained are highly relevant matching the requirements set forth by both the jobseekers and employers.
